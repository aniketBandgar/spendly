Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account (such as your name and email address) and the expense data you enter while using Spendly. We do not collect payment information or sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and personalise your experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, alteration, or deletion. However, no method of storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and analytics. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for purposes other than providing services to us.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 5 business days.
